# Wireframe Notes

## Wireframe Status

No UI should be built yet. These notes define what future wireframes should explore.

## Dashboard Wireframe Goals

- Show a concise summary of renewal workload.
- Place urgent renewals and pending approvals where users notice them quickly.
- Use a restrained SaaS dashboard layout with clear hierarchy.
- Avoid a marketing-style landing page.

Potential modules:

- Priority renewal queue preview.
- High-risk renewals.
- Pending approvals.
- Savings opportunities.
- Renewal timeline or upcoming deadline band.

## Renewal Queue Wireframe Goals

- Support fast scanning and comparison.
- Make owners, dates, risk, and approval states visible.
- Provide a clear primary action for each item.
- Keep filtering simple and familiar.

Potential columns:

- Subscription.
- Owner.
- Renewal date.
- Annual spend.
- Usage signal.
- Risk.
- Approval status.
- Next action.

## Subscription Detail Wireframe Goals

- Start with a summary panel for key facts.
- Group related details into sections.
- Show decision actions near the context needed to choose them.
- Use confirmation for high-impact choices.

Potential sections:

- Summary.
- Timeline.
- Usage.
- Spend.
- Ownership.
- Notes.
- Decision history.

## Approval Flow Wireframe Goals

- Show what decision is being requested.
- Make the consequence of the action clear.
- Avoid accidental approval or cancellation.
- End with a clear confirmation state.

## UX Laws to Reference

- Hick's Law for limiting decision choices.
- Fitts's Law for placement of primary actions.
- Miller's Law for grouping dense information.
- Law of Common Region for section structure.
- Error Prevention for confirmation patterns.
