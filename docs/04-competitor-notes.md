# Competitor Notes

## Status

This file is a structured placeholder for competitor and pattern analysis. It does not claim a completed market audit.

## Relevant Product Categories

- SaaS spend management platforms.
- Procurement and vendor management tools.
- Subscription tracking tools.
- Contract lifecycle management tools.
- Finance operations dashboards.

## Patterns to Study

- Dashboard summaries for spend, risk, and upcoming work.
- Renewal calendars and deadline-based queues.
- Owner assignment and approval routing.
- Risk labels, urgency states, and exception handling.
- Savings opportunity cards and recommendation patterns.
- Detail pages that combine contract, usage, spend, and decision context.

## Questions for Competitive Review

- How do similar tools prioritize urgent renewal work?
- What information appears in list views versus detail views?
- How are owners, approvers, and decision states represented?
- How do products distinguish risk from opportunity?
- What confirmation patterns are used for high-impact decisions?
- Which dashboard patterns feel familiar and trustworthy for B2B users?

## UX Principles to Apply

- Jakob's Law: use familiar SaaS conventions where they reduce learning effort.
- Von Restorff Effect: make truly important exceptions stand out.
- Progressive Disclosure: avoid forcing every contract detail into the first view.
- Recognition over Recall: surface key facts directly in the interface.

## Portfolio Note

Any future competitive references should be cited clearly and described as pattern inspiration, not as copied product behavior.
